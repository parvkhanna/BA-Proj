Business Analyst Showcase: CRM System Development

Project Overview

Title: Development of a CRM System for ABC Corp Sales Department

Objective: Create a CRM system to help the sales department manage leads, track customer interactions, and improve sales performance.

Role: Business Analyst

Duration: 5 months

Team: 1 BA, 4 Developers, 2 QA, 1 Project Manager, 3 Key Stakeholders

1. Initiation and Stakeholder Analysis

Goal: Understand the needs of the sales and marketing teams. Stakeholders include sales managers, marketing heads, customer support, and the IT department.

  • Conducted stakeholder interviews to gather goals such as improving lead management and tracking customer touchpoints.
  • Created a Stakeholder Analysis document.
2. Requirement Gathering and Documentation

Tools: Jira for tracking requirements, Confluence for collaboration.

  • Captured key functionalities in a Business Requirement Document (BRD).
  • Defined processes in a Functional Requirement Specification (FRS).
  • Included non-functional requirements for performance, security, and scalability.
3. Business Process Modeling

Tools: Lucidchart, Microsoft Visio.

  • Mapped existing and optimized sales processes in As-Is and To-Be diagrams.
  • Created use case diagrams illustrating key interactions between users and the CRM system.
4. Gap Analysis

Conducted a Gap Analysis to identify inefficiencies and proposed automation via the CRM.

  • Prioritized features such as lead follow-up automation, sales forecasting, and task reminders.
5. Solution Design

Tools: Figma for UI/UX design, Jira for tracking user stories.

  • Created wireframes and mockups for the CRMâ€™s main interface.
  • Defined user stories for each functionality.
6. Testing and Validation

Developed Test Cases and UAT Scenarios, collaborated with the QA team, and performed system testing and UAT.

7. Implementation and User Training

Conducted training sessions for sales reps and managers, and created user manuals for CRM adoption.

8. Post-Implementation Support and Maintenance

Provided ongoing support, collaborated with IT for CRM updates, and ensured the system addressed evolving business needs.

Key Project Responsibilities as a Business Analyst

  • Stakeholder Engagement: Regular meetings with departments to align system goals.
  • Requirement Gathering: Interviewed stakeholders and documented requirements.
  • Process Improvement: Mapped and optimized sales workflows.
  • Gap Analysis: Identified process inefficiencies and prioritized CRM features.
  • Solution Design: Collaborated with UX designers and developers.
  • Testing: Created UAT scenarios and facilitated testing.
  • Training and Support: Created user manuals and provided ongoing support.

Project Outcomes

  • Reduced manual lead tracking by 40%, enabling more focus on customer relationships.
  • Increased lead conversion rates by 25% through automated reminders.
  • Improved sales pipeline visibility and more accurate sales forecasts.
  • Better customer retention and satisfaction due to accurate customer data.
